Meet Our Team

Meet the Visionaries Behind Jungsik

At Jungsik, our team is driven by a passion for reimagining traditional Korean flavors through a luxurious, contemporary lens. Led by our executive chef, who blends age-old techniques with modern culinary innovation, each member of our team brings a unique expertise to the table. From our seasoned sommeliers who pair the finest wines with bold, authentic dishes, to our dedicated service staff who ensure an unforgettable dining experience, everyone at Jungsik is committed to excellence. We believe that true luxury lies in the details, and our team is devoted to crafting moments that make each visit a celebration of Korean culture and flavor.
