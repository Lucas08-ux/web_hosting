The Essence of Korean Luxury

At Jungsik, we honor Korea’s culinary heritage while embracing innovation. Every dish is crafted to delight your senses, offering a harmonious balance of flavors that celebrate our roots and elevate tradition to fine dining.

Our philosophy centers on creating an unforgettable culinary journey, where each plate tells a story of Korea's vibrant flavors and refined techniques. With an emphasis on seasonal ingredients and artistic presentation, we transform familiar dishes into something extraordinary, inviting our guests to explore the depth and elegance of Korean cuisine in a truly elevated setting.

Discover the Atmosphere

Our Dining Room

Experience a space where tradition meets contemporary elegance. The dining room is designed to create a serene atmosphere, perfect for enjoying Korean cuisine. Minimalist decor and refined lighting enhance the experience, offering a warm setting for both intimate and celebratory occasions.

The Exterior

The exterior blends modernity with heritage, reflecting our culinary philosophy. Clean lines and understated design offer a striking first impression, while the surrounding greenery provides a peaceful retreat, transitioning from the lively city to a space of refined luxury.

Jin-Soo Park 박진수

Jin-Soo Park, founder of Jungsik, grew up in Busan, South Korea, surrounded by the flavors and traditions of Korean cuisine. With roots in a family of passionate cooks, he learned the art of crafting authentic dishes from a young age.

Now, after honing his skills in prestigious kitchens in Seoul and Paris, Jin-Soo brings a refined approach to traditional Korean flavors. His vision is to blend heritage with innovation, creating a unique dining experience that celebrates Korea’s culinary artistry at the highest level.

Michelin-Starred Excellence

Jungsik is honored to hold two Michelin stars, a testament to its dedication to excellence in Korean fine dining. Every dish at Jungsik embodies the balance of tradition and innovation, meticulously crafted to captivate the senses. These Michelin stars recognize not only the mastery of our chefs but also the commitment to authenticity, quality, and artistry that define every plate served.

Achieving two Michelin stars is both a recognition and a responsibility. At Jungsik, we continuously strive to exceed expectations, refining techniques and exploring bold flavors that elevate Korean cuisine to new heights. Our team views each dish as a tribute to Korea's culinary heritage, aiming to offer an unforgettable dining experience that honors and redefines tradition in every bite.
