The Essence of Flavor

Indulge in our carefully crafted tasting experiences, where each dish is a celebration of Korean tradition and modern innovation. Our first menu is designed for light diners who seek refined flavors, offering a selection of five dishes and two signature desserts that embody the essence of Korean culinary artistry.

For a more generous dining experience, our second menu presents a curated feast of seven courses and two desserts, perfect for those with a hearty appetite. This menu embraces a deeper exploration of flavors, textures, and ingredients, showcasing the finest of Korean cuisine for a truly memorable dining journey.

Exclusive Tasting Menus

Light Tasting Menu

For those who prefer a lighter experience

5 Courses + 2 Desserts

Appetizers

HWE 회

Fresh Fish Carpaccio with thinly sliced fresh fish, such as sea bream or yellowtail, served with gochujang, vinegar, and sesame. A delicate, ocean-inspired starter.

Japchae 잡채

Sweet Potato Noodles stir-fried with fresh vegetables and thin slices of marinated beef, in a light soy-sesame dressing. Medium portion.

Samgyeopsal 삼겹살

Slices of grilled pork belly, served with ssamjang sauce and lettuce for wrapping. Moderate portion.

Main Courses

Galbijjim 갈비찜

Braised Beef Short Ribs slow-cooked in a soy-ginger sauce, yielding tender meat. Small serving to highlight deep, rich flavors.

Bibimbap 비빔밥

A small portion of mixed rice topped with spinach, carrots, sprouts, and egg, served with gochujang sauce on the side. A fresh mix of textures and flavors.

Desserts

Patbingsu 팽빙수

Shaved Ice topped with sweet red bean paste and fresh seasonal fruits. Refreshing and lightly sweet.

Yakgwa 약과

A small, flower-shaped honey cookie made with wheat flour, fried, and finished with sesame oil. Rich and subtly sweet, a true taste of tradition.

Grand Tasting Menu

For a more abundant culinary journey

6 Unique Courses + 2 Unique Desserts

Appetizers

Bulgogi 불고기

Thin slices of marinated beef, grilled to perfection. A slightly larger portion, balancing savory and sweet flavors.

Kongnamul Gukbap 공날름 국밥

A light and refreshing soybean sprout soup served with rice. Known for its cleansing qualities and mild flavor.

Yangnyeom Tongdak 양념 통닭

Korean-Style Fried Chicken coated in a sticky, spicy-sweet sauce. Crisp texture with a burst of flavor.

Main Courses

Sannakji 산낙지

Live Octopus served fresh with a light sesame oil and salt dipping sauce. A traditional Korean delicacy with an adventurous texture.

Bossam 보쌈

Boiled Pork Belly with spicy kimchi wraps and radish. Medium portion with a rich and savory profile.

Doenjang Jjigae 된장찌개

A hearty, earthy stew made with fermented soybean paste, tofu, mushrooms, and vegetables. Small serving for a savory depth.

Desserts

Injeolmi Bingsu 인절미 빙수

Shaved Ice topped with sweet rice cakes and nutty soybean powder. A delightful contrast of textures.

Hotteok 호떡

A warm, golden pancake filled with brown sugar and chopped nuts. A comforting, sweet end to the meal.
